Meet KOMAL

Knowledge-Oriented Mental-Health & Affective Learning

A mobile and web-based application with hyperpersonalized virtual humans for social-emotional learning (SEL) for users aged 3 to 12. The platform features virtual avatars inspired by social robotics research and socially assistive technologies for children with neurodiverse conditions.
